Privacy Policy for MediHydrate

Effective Date: January 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MediHydrate ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • User Profile: Name (optional) for personalized greetings
  • Health Data:
    • Water intake goals and consumption records
    • Medication names, dosages, schedules, and stock levels
    • Medical appointment details (doctor names, locations, dates)
    • Medical reports and documents you choose to upload
  • Preferences: Notification settings, measurement units, reminder intervals

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage Data: Features used, interaction patterns, crash reports
  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the MediHydrate app functionality:

  • Display your hydration progress and statistics
  • Send medication, water, and appointment reminders
  • Generate health reports and analytics
  • Provide motivational quotes and personalized messages
  • Save and display your medical documents
  • Customize the app experience based on your preferences

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. MediHydrate does not transmit or store your personal health information on external servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • Data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device
  • Medical documents and reports are saved in your device's local storage
  • No cloud synchronization or external data transmission
  • Data is accessible only through the MediHydrate app on your device

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • No data is shared with analytics providers
  • No data is shared with advertising networks
  • No data is accessible to other apps without your explicit action

6. Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual records or clear all data through app settings
  • Export: Export your data through the app's export features
  • Portability: Your data remains on your device and moves with it

7. Permissions

MediHydrate may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send reminders for medications, water intake, and appointments
  • Storage: To save and access medical reports and documents
  • Exact Alarm (Android): To schedule precise medication reminders

You can manage these permissions through your device settings at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

MediHydrate is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Backup and Recovery

  • Your data is not automatically backed up to cloud services
  • Device backups (iOS/Android) may include app data based on your device settings
  • We recommend regularly exporting important data using the app's export features

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
