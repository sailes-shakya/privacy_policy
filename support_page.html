How do I set up water reminders? +

To set up water reminders:

  1. Go to Settings from the home screen
  2. Tap on "Hydration Settings"
  3. Toggle "Enable Water Reminders" to ON
  4. Set your preferred reminder interval (15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes)
  5. Choose your active hours for reminders
How do I add a medication reminder? +

To add a medication reminder:

  1. Tap the + button on the home screen
  2. Select "Medication" from the options
  3. Enter medication name and dosage
  4. Set reminder times (you can add multiple times per day)
  5. Enter current stock and refill alert threshold
  6. Save your medication
Why am I not receiving notifications? +

If you're not receiving notifications:

  1. Check your device's notification settings for MediHydrate
  2. Ensure notifications are enabled in the app settings
  3. For Android: Grant "Exact Alarm" permission in system settings
  4. Make sure Do Not Disturb mode is not blocking notifications
  5. Try restarting the app or your device
How do I export my health data? +

To export your health data:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Tap on "Data & Privacy"
  3. Select "Export Data"
  4. Choose the date range and data types to export
  5. Select export format (PDF or CSV)
  6. Share or save the exported file
Is my health data secure? +

Yes! Your health data is completely secure:

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No cloud synchronization without your explicit action
  • You have full control to delete your data anytime
  • Data is protected by your device's security measures
How do I set up the morning motivation alarm? +

To set up your morning motivation alarm:

  1. On the home screen, find the "Morning Motivation" toggle
  2. Tap on it to access settings
  3. Set your preferred wake-up time (4 AM - 11 AM)
  4. Toggle the alarm ON
  5. You'll receive a daily motivational health quote at your chosen time

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

  1. App crashes on startup:
    • Clear app cache in device settings
    • Restart your device
    • Reinstall the app (your data will be preserved)
  2. Water intake not saving:
    • Check if you have sufficient storage space
    • Try logging a different amount
    • Restart the app
  3. Medication reminders not working:
    • Verify the medication is active (not paused)
    • Check reminder times are correctly set
    • Ensure notification permissions are granted
  4. Reports not generating:
    • Ensure you have data for the selected period
    • Try a different date range
    • Check storage permissions for PDF export
